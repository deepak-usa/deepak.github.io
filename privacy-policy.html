Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Welcome to Deepak World. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect and handle your information when you use our website or Android applications.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and phone number when you register or contact us.
  • Transaction Data: Payment details processed securely via PhonePe and Razorpay (we do not store full card/PIN details).
  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and security.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our games, including levels cleared and in-game achievements.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services/games.
  • Process your donations and payments via secured payment gateways.
  • Deliver instant digital game downloads.
  • Notify you about updates or technical issues.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your data. Information is only shared with trusted third parties like PhonePe and Razorpay for payment processing. We use industry-standard encryption and reasonable security practices to protect your data.

4. Advertising & Cookies

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

  • How Google uses data: Google uses advertising cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • How to opt-out: You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info. You may also manage your cookie preferences through the consent banner displayed on our site.

5. Your Rights (India DPDP Act 2026 Compliance)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate personal information.
  • Request the erasure of your data when no longer needed.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are for children, but we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without parental consent. We comply with Google Play's "Teacher Approved" and "Families" policies regarding data collection.
