Project & Payment Flow

1. Requirement Gathering

Client shares project details (type of design: ID card, report card, invitation, poster, etc.).

We discuss specifications such as size, colors, branding, and delivery format.

A quotation is provided based on scope and complexity.

2. Confirmation & Advance Payment

Once the client approves the quotation, an advance payment (usually 50%) is made to initiate the project.

Payment can be made via bank transfer, UPI, or other accepted methods.

3. Design & Draft Sharing

Our team creates the first draft based on client requirements.

Drafts are shared for review and feedback.

Clients can request revisions (within agreed limits).

4. Finalization

After revisions, the final design is prepared.

High-quality files (PDF, PNG, JPG, or print-ready formats) are generated.

5. Final Payment

The remaining balance is paid before delivery of the final files.

Invoice and payment receipt are provided.

6. Delivery

Final designs are delivered digitally via email, cloud link, or WhatsApp.

For print projects, delivery/shipping options are discussed separately.

7. Post-Delivery Support

Minor edits (like spelling corrections) are offered within a short grace period.

Long-term clients enjoy priority support for future projects.

This flow makes your process transparent and professional, reassuring clients that their project will be handled smoothly from source to delivery.
